# Microsoft OneNote
- Half infinite canvas (bottom and right)
  - Format background
- Toolbar position: top, bottom, left, right
- Pens
  - Rainbow, Galaxy, ...
  - Highlighter
  - Fountain pen
  - Brush pen
  - Laser pointer
  - Eyedropper
- Ruler
- Shapes
  - Automatic shapes
- Text
  - Ink to text
  - OCR
- Math
  - Ink to math
- Lasso
  - Cut, copy, paste
  - Can be triggered by touch, often too easily
- Ink replay

Overall more powerful than [Whiteboard](Whiteboard.md).
